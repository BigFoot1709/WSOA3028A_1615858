Game 1 - Gibbs and Gayle Fireworks Show


Chris Gayle’s amazing 117 runs coming off just 57 balls. Video Source: Entertainment. 24 - edited by Devan Gray.

Tonight’s match started with Chris Gayle’s blazing century – the first century ever scored in the new Twenty20 format of the game – that looked to lock in the West Indies as sure winners. But was not the case as a quick fire 90 from Herschelle Gibbs, helped with some poor fielding and bowling, helped see South Africa home with 2 whole overs to spare. West Indies dropped 3 catches and bowled a surprising 23 wides that didn’t do them any good.
