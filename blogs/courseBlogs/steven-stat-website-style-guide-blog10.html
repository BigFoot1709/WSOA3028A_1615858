Reasons Behind Design Decisions

The colour palette I chose had the colours displayed above to try and match that of modern coding environment/console but at the same time try and have a retro old school kind of feel to it. The character’s stories are about stats and numbers so I thought of having that computer feel would go well with the numbers playing displayed. With a darker heavier background to surround the much lighter coloured numbers to make them stand out more.

The font style I chose has the name Roboto and I chose it because I thought it had a clean cut and easy to read feel to it and was great for headings and text in the body text example. Easy readability in larger paragraphs would be seen with this font style. The bold and italic styles of the font also have a clean cut to it and make for easy readability but still at the same time have their demarcations clear. Headings for blogs/stories will be located in more central positions in the future to catch the eye a bit more. Blog/story titles on the home page will also be changed in the future to effect more “popping out” to the user with the scale and emphasis of the heading being changed.

For the buttons I thought it would be a good idea to make sure the buttons animate in colour change when the user interacts with them in some way (such as hovering over them or click on them with the mouse) to give a little more interaction with the website. I also plan for all buttons on the website to be the same design and exact size as to not create too much bias between them. Buttons will be used mainly in navigation around the beside such as in the navigation bar for example. The navigation bar will be more structured in the future with more balance and alignment with the other elements of the website being displayed.

Reflection on changed Designs from Wireframes and Style Guide

From the displayed style guide and planned wireframes from above I think most of the planned designs were implemented. The main font used everywhere on the website works very well as a very easy to read font and works well for headings. Most of the colours from the colour palette were used with the green (#428C3F) not being used as through the building of the website it did not feel like it would fit anywhere. Simplified buttons were used as seen as they are now on the website as the detailed buttons in the style guide were too detailed to fit on the website as it is now. It was also decided to make the background of the website for all pages a much darker blue then planned as it gave the website a more “dark theme” to be easier on the eye.

For the wireframes, through the building of the website the general layout of the planned wireframes was followed with small details being missed due to lack of time. For example, the side bars on the wireframes for the Blogs/StatStories Pages and the Individual Blogs/StatStories Pages were originally planned for ads of some type that would fit the idea of the personal website, but we not used. As well as the planned list of other blogs and StatStories on the left of the wireframe for the Individual Blogs/StatStories Pages not being used. The closed implemented pages to their original plans have to be the Home Page and the Blogs/StatStories Pages. It was through the actual building and making of the website where plans where changed through the trying of new and different things to see if it would stick.
